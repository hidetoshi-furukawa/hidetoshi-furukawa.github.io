Autoware

Autoware.AI is the world’s first “All-in-One” open-source software for autonomous driving technology.

Hidetoshi Furukawa
May 8, 2020 1 min read

Autoware is an autonomous driving software developed by Autoware Fundation.

There are three projects: Autoware.AI, Autoware.IO, and Autoware.Auto.

Autoware.AI built on ROS 1 is available on Gitlab, Autoware.Auto built on ROS 2 is also available on Gitlab.
